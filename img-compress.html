How to Compress Images Online

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload button
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher values = better quality)
  3. Choose your desired output format (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  4. Optionally resize your image by enabling the resize option
  5. Click "Compress Image" and wait a moment for processing
  6. Download your optimized image with reduced file size

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading times (improves SEO ranking)
  • Reduced bandwidth usage and server load
  • Better user experience on mobile devices
  • Easier sharing via email or messaging apps
  • Optimized storage space for your images
